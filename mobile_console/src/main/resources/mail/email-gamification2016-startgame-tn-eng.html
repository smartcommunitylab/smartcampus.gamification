Earn points is easy:
- Plan your trips with ViaggiaTrento Play&Go ("Planning trip" in home);
- Save your itinerary ("Save Trip" in the details page);
- Remember to plot your path when you run the trip ("Start" and "Stop" in trip details in "My trips");

Each trip lets you earn Green Leaves points in proportion with the kms done in a sustainable mode (walking, by byke/bike sharing, with public trasport).

To see your complete results and the updated rankings see the app ViaggaTrento in the Play&Go section.
